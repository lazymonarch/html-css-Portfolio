My Projects

APTS

The "APTS (Ambulance Prioritise Traffic System)" is a project designed to help ambulances navigate traffic during emergencies. Using a sound sensor, the system detects whether an ambulance is in emergency mode, while image recognition confirms the vehicle's identity. When an ambulance approaches a red traffic light, the system detects it and changes the light to green, allowing it to pass through safely. This ensures quicker response times for emergency services and reduces delays caused by traffic congestion. The system seamlessly prioritizes ambulance passage, improving efficiency in critical situations.

Medkonnect

"Medkonnect" is a blockchain-based app designed to securely connect patients with doctors, ensuring the discreet transfer of medical information. The app leverages blockchain technology to protect sensitive data, offering enhanced privacy and transparency. Patients can easily share their health records with trusted healthcare providers, ensuring their medical details remain confidential and tamper-proof. The app allows doctors to access up-to-date information, helping in better diagnosis and treatment. With a focus on privacy and security, "Medkonnect" streamlines the process of medical data exchange, empowering patients to take control of their healthcare.

AidLink

"Link" is a disaster response app designed to help people in emergencies. Once users log in, they can push a large red button to quickly notify emergency services like the police, ambulance, or fire department. The app is built to streamline communication in critical situations, making it easier for users to call for help with just one click. It ensures that assistance is dispatched immediately, whether it's a medical emergency, fire, or other disaster. The app's user-friendly interface is designed to be simple and effective during high-stress moments.
